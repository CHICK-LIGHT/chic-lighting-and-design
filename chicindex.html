about chic lighing

At Chic Lighting, we believe lighting is more than just illumination — it shapes mood, enhances design, and transforms spaces beautifully. We provide modern, elegant, and energy-efficient lighting solutions for homes, offices, and commercial interiors.

Chic Lighting & Design has its presence since 1971. We offer unique lighting and accessories from the most elegant traditional to refined contemporary for today’s eclectic interiors. Chic Lighting & Design is an online retailer that specializes in LED Lights, Lamps, Chandeliers and Pendant lighting. We have a range of collections that include; crystal chandeliers, traditional chandeliers, modern chandeliers, glass pendant lights, industrial and retro pendant lights, metal pendant lights and many more. At Chic Lighting & Design, we are dedicated to customer service. When you call or email during normal business hours, you will speak or get reply from a design professional. Our knowledgeable and experienced staff receives ongoing education and training and is available to assist with all of your lighting projects.

From contemporary chandeliers to smart LED solutions, we offer a curated selection of products that combine aesthetics with functionality.
